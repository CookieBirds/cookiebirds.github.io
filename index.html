About The Game

Cookie Birds is a fun crypto NFTs game with a Lots of social activities, mini games and Challenges.
Join the new metaverse experiance with your unique NFT Birds, Each NFT you mint is a unique 3D model that you can view, play with, Upgrade and trade. Total NFTs are limitied to 10,000 NFTs, Once they all minited, No one can mint more.

Why Cookie Birds NFTs ??

Your NFT is not just a useless garbage images on the name of (art) that just depending on a memes without any real value, Cookie Birds NFTs are a unique 3D models with different abilities that you can actually move and play with in the metaverse.

Cookie birds Features

There are 10 species of NFT birds, Each species has 8 unique parts.
When you mint a bird you will get a random 8 parts from all the 10 birds.

The more similar parts you get the more rare your bird will be.
Read more about rarity here.

Multiple gaming methods will be available (campaign, Survival, rece, Challenges ... more comming soon).
Soon you will be able to level up your birds and unloack new fecutures.
