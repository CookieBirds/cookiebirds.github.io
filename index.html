About The Game

Cookie Birds is fun Free-to-play/play to earn game with a Lots of levels and Challenges.

Features

  • Great 3D Graphics With Post-Processing effects.
  • Lots of levels, More coming soon.
  • A variety of birds to choose from, Each bird has different abilities.
  • multiple gaming mods(campaign, Survival, rece, Challenges ... more comming soon)
  • Choose your favorite bird and have fun.

Gameplay

Your bird can fly up, left and right. Flying up will cost you stamina. If you run out of stamina you will not gonna be able to fly, You can restore your stamina by stepping on the ground or any safe floor. Follow the gold to know where to go. Have fun and try to reach to the winning station.
